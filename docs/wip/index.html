Understanding Reactivity

– December 15, 2025

In late 2023, staring at the “flag” test case in preact-signals that I describe in my Archaeology of Glitches posting, I realised something decisive: reactive systems can provide a strong, structural guarantee of data consistency. Given that, it became clear that Infusion could be reworked as a fully reactive system, rather than the opportunistic hybrid I had been building through the 2010s. This post will notice that the apparent diversity of reactive systems masks a simple unifying principle, related to a principle I had already been following in Infusion’s development. [Read More]
